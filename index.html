Recent Projects

Hospital Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) System

Delivered a comprehensive front-end solution for a hospital's Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) system as part of a client project. The system streamlined patient registration and automated the health insurance data collation process. A major focus was reducing the post-discharge insurance claim settlement time—from 8–9 hours down to just 2 hours—by automating communication between the hospital and insurance providers. The solution significantly improved the discharge experience for patients and their families while increasing operational efficiency for the hospital.

Intelligent Invoice Management System (IIMS)

Built a front-end interface for the Intelligent Invoice Management System (IIMS) to digitize and streamline end-to-end invoice handling. The system automates the conversion of physical invoices into digital format using Google OCR, extracting key details such as invoice amount, vendor information, and PO numbers. It performs GST and tax verification via government APIs, ensuring data accuracy and compliance. Invoices are then matched with purchase orders, reviewed, approved by managers, and securely stored in a cloud/database for further processing and audit readiness. This solution reduced manual workload, improved accuracy, and sped up the overall invoice cycle.

Vendor Management System (VMS)

Developed and integrated interactive reporting tools into a Vendor Management System (VMS) to track vendor performance, monitor KPIs, and support strategic decision-making. The system provides a clear and organized view of all vendors, making it easy to manage multiple relationships and assess performance trends over time. It also helps track vendor licenses and compliance status, reducing operational risks and ensuring regulatory alignment. This solution empowered stakeholders with data-driven insights to plan next-step strategies and maintain a more stable and efficient supply chain.

Inventory Management System (IMS)

Developed real-time, interactive dashboards for an Inventory Management System (IMS) using React.js, providing clear visibility into stock levels and invoice tracking. The system helped prevent both shortages and overstocking by enabling timely reorders and optimizing inventory flow. It also supported better financial decision-making through integrated analytics and invoice monitoring. Additional benefits included improved demand forecasting, reduced holding costs, and enhanced coordination with suppliers. This solution significantly improved operational efficiency and ensured that inventory was aligned with business needs at all times.

Role-Based E-Commerce Platform with Digital Product Support

Built a fully responsive e-commerce website designed for both desktop and mobile users, enhancing user engagement and improving conversion rates. The platform features role-based login for Admins and Users. Admins can manage product listings, create multiple offer packages, and update promotional content. Users can browse products, add items to cart or wishlist, and view their purchase history complete with downloadable bills and delivery details. The system also supports digital products like software, posters, images, and videos. With a clean, user-friendly interface and seamless functionality across all devices, the platform provides a complete and scalable online shopping experience.

Integrated Billing & Table Booking System with Online Menu for Restaurants and Cafés

Developed a fully responsive Billing and Table Booking System that empowers restaurants and cafés to operate efficiently with fewer staff. Customers can browse the online menu, book tables, place orders, and settle bills directly from any device—whether mobile, tablet, or desktop—enhancing convenience and reducing wait times. The system automates order management and billing, streamlining operations and improving customer experience. Its responsive design ensures seamless usability across all device types, making it a versatile solution for modern hospitality businesses.
